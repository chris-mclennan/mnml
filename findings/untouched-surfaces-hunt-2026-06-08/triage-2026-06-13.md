### 2. `shell_exec` reader threads have no timeout

**Status: open.** `src/ai/api_client.rs` still joins
`stdout_join` / `stderr_join` without a deadline. The 60s child
timeout is real but a backgrounded grandchild (`sleep 9999 &`)
keeps the pipe alive past parent exit, so the joins block
indefinitely.

Bounded fix: replace the blocking joins with a deadline-based
read using a `mpsc::channel` send from the reader thread + a
`recv_timeout` on the main thread. ~30 lines.
